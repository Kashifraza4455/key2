INTRODUCTION OF PAKISTAN

Capital and Major Cities: The capital of Pakistan is Islamabad. Other major cities include Karachi, Lahore, Faisalabad, and Rawalpindi. Languages: The official language of Pakistan is Urdu. However, there are several other languages spoken throughout the country, including Punjabi, Sindhi, Pashto, and Balochi.Religion: Islam is the predominant religion in Pakistan, with the majority of the population being Sunni Muslims.There is also a significant Shia Muslim minority.Government: Pakistan is a federal parliamentary republic.The President is the head of state, while the Prime Minister is the head of government. Pakistan has a mixed economy with agriculture, manufacturing,and services sectors. Major industries include textiles, agriculture, manufacturing, and information technology. Pakistani culture is diverse and influenced by various ethnic, linguistic, and religious groups. The country has a rich history of literature, music, art, and poetry.Pakistan has a range of educational institutions, including universities and schools.However, there are challenges in terms of access to quality education and literacy rates The country is characterized by a diverse landscape, including mountains, deserts, plains, and a coastline along the Arabian Sea. The northern areas are home to some of the world's highest peaks, including K2.Pakistan has a lot to offer in terms of tourism, including historical sites like Mohenjo-daro and Taxila, natural beauty in places like the Swat Valley and Gilgit-Baltistan region, and cultural experiences in cities like Lahore. Pakistan faces various challenges, including political instability, security concerns, poverty, healthcare issues, and environmental problems

POPULATION OF PAKISTAN
